Exercise

  • Warm up

    Before starting any exercise it is very neccesary to get warmed up and all the blood circulation should start.

  • SideLeg-Rise

    Side leg raise is an exercise that targets the muscles in your hips and outer thighs. Side leg raises can help tone and sculpt the muscles in your hips and outer thighs, giving you a more defined and shapely lower body.

  • Spot Jogging

    Spot jogging, also known as stationary jogging, is a low-impact cardio exercise that involves jogging in place. : Spot jogging is a great cardiovascular exercise that can help increase your heart rate and improve your lung capacity. This, in turn, can improve your overall health and fitness.

  • Towel Streching

    Towel stretching is a stretching technique that involves using a towel to help stretch your muscles Towel stretching can help improve your overall flexibility by stretching your muscles and increasing your range of motion.
